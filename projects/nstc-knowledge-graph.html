Project Detail

NSTC Research: AI + Knowledge Graph

This undergraduate research project explored how to build standard procedures for chronic disease health-education knowledge graph construction using web scraping and large language models.

Research Focus

Knowledge Graph Workflow

Designed standardized steps for data collection, normalization, and graph structuring.

LLM-assisted Processing

Used LLM support to improve information extraction and semantic mapping quality.

Conference Citation

T.-H. Huang, C.-C. Lo, and D.-R. Tai, "Design and study of standard procedures for building a chronic disease health-education knowledge graph system based on web scraping and large language models," in 2024 Annual Conference and Academic Symposium of the Chinese Institute of Industrial Engineers, Nov. 30, 2024.
